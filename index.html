Frequently asked question

What areas do you specialise in?

We currently have a team of 10 designers which specializes in wide range of design services. from Logo to poster design to cool designs for your merchandise.

Can cancel my poject at any time?

Your project can only be canceled before the final phrase. After the design has been finalized and full payment has been made, your project can't be canceled.

In what time frame will I receive my work?

Our services takes a minimum of 2 days and a maximum of 5 days to deliver, depending on the job offered.

Can I pay after I get my design?

No. A full payment should be made before you can get your final design. But we'll be updating you on the process consistently.

What if I don't like the design?

With the way our design process is structured, we'll take you through all the steps from the beginning so early changes can be made.
